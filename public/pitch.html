THE PROBLEM

A nurse in rural Malawi says
"running stomach" into her phone.
Cloud AI returns nothing.

No Internet

Disaster zones and rural areas have zero connectivity. Cloud-based AI tools are dead on arrival.

Language Barrier

"Running stomach" → Gastroenteritis. "Hot body" → Hyperpyrexia. Clinical NLP fails on colloquial terms.

Cloud Cost

4 API subscriptions + satellite uplink + HIPAA compliance overhead = thousands of dollars per month.

Paint the scene: rural health worker, no internet, colloquial medical language. Cloud AI requires internet, uses generic NLP, and costs thousands. SomaPulse solves all three.
THE SOLUTION

Clinical AI that runs on a single laptop.

🎤 Edge Transcription

Whisper.cpp compiled to C++ — 38ms p50 transcription on CPU. No GPU, no cloud.

🧬 SapBERT Mapping

Biomedical ONNX model maps colloquial terms to UMLS clinical concepts with 91%+ similarity.

💊 Protocol Retrieval

sqlite-vec cosine search against 142 pre-indexed medical protocols. Action steps + contraindications.

Three-stage pipeline: Whisper transcribes speech, SapBERT maps to clinical concepts, sqlite-vec retrieves matching protocols. Total pipeline: under 120ms.
